About Me

2012–2016: Gymnasium No. 323 with in-depth study of English


My educational journey began at Gymnasium No. 323, where I studied from grades 1 to 4. It was a school with intensive English language studies, which became an important foundation for my further development. From the very first years of my education, I became familiar with a foreign language not only at a basic level, but also through classes aimed at developing conversational skills, reading, and writing. Early contact with the English language allowed me to develop confidence in its use and sparked an interest in international communication. This period was not only the beginning of my academic journey, but also the first step in shaping my interests in information analysis, global thinking, and the pursuit of continuous development.

2016–2023: Lyceum “Naukova Zmina”


From 5th to 11th grade, I studied at one of Kyiv's leading educational institutions, the Naukova Zmina Lyceum, which is known for its high academic standards and innovative approach to education. I was a member of the technology group, where special attention was paid to in-depth study of mathematics, natural sciences, and information technology. Here, I not only mastered fundamental disciplines such as Pascal, HTML, and CSS at the beginner level, but also began to apply the knowledge I had gained in practice — solving complex problems, working on projects, and participating in competitions and specialized events. This stage was key in shaping my thinking and laying a solid foundation in computer science. It was at the lyceum that I developed a clear understanding that I wanted to pursue a career in IT and engineering, and my desire for continuous self-improvement was strengthened.

2023 – present: Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv


Since 2023, I have been studying at one of the most prestigious universities in Ukraine — Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, at the Faculty of Informatics and Cybernetics, where I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in Informatics. During my years of study, I successfully mastered basic and advanced disciplines, including algorithms, data structures, object-oriented programming, mathematical analysis, and theoretical computer science. These courses not only strengthened my academic background but also helped me better understand the applied side of computer science. The university environment encourages me to constantly develop — I actively participate in projects, solve competition and research problems, and study modern approaches to data analysis and software engineering. My education here has been a logical continuation of my interest in technology and has laid the foundation for further professional growth in the field of data analysis, programming, and digital products. Now, I finished the second year and have a great desire to discover myself, through the process of creating lots of projects.
